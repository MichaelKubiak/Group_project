Welcome to the BIOLOGY=TRUE website!

Herein lies the world of single cell RNA sequencing (scRNAseq), the joys of 3D interactive graphs and A WORKING GENE SEARCH BAR.
Your life will never be the same again.

Our work is based on the scRNAseq data and analysis of Darmanis et al. (2015) , who located 10 distinct cell types via the clustering of 466 brain cells.
We have replicated their methodology to identify whether the their results are reproducible.
Additional pipelines have been located to see whether the use of different methodologies can still reproduce the same results. Graphs produced by the replicated methodology and our new pipeline can be seen from the 'Original Pipeline' and Newer Pipeline' dropdown menus respectively.
The report containing our findings can also be seen within the 'Report' page.
To search our database of all of the genes used within the report, please select the 'Query Genes' tab.
We hope you enjoy your stay.
